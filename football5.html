# MORE ABOUT LUKA MODRIC:-



SATED BY LUKA MODRIC :- "Don't cry because it's over, smile because it happened,"

Luka Modrić is a Croatian professional footballer who plays as a central midfielder and captains both Real Madrid and the Croatia national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest midfielders of all time and the greatest Croatian footballer. Modrić is known for his exceptional playmaking abilities, vision, and dribbling skills. Early Life and Career: Born on September 9, 1985, in Zadar, Croatia. Began his professional career with Dinamo Zagreb, also playing on loan at Zrinjski Mostar and Inter Zaprešić. Signed with Tottenham Hotspur in 2008, where he spent four seasons before moving to Real Madrid in 2012. Real Madrid Career: Joined Real Madrid in 2012 and has become a key player for the club. Won numerous titles with Real Madrid, including: Six UEFA Champions League titles. Four La Liga titles. Two Copa del Rey titles. Four FIFA Club World Cup titles. Captained the team in recent seasons. International Career: Captained the Croatian national team and is the most-capped player with over 170 appearances. Led Croatia to the 2018 FIFA World Cup Final, where they finished as runners-up. Won the Golden Ball for the best player at the 2018 World Cup. Has participated in numerous major tournaments for Croatia, including the UEFA European Championship and the FIFA World Cup.
